# Prashant Tewari

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Haifa studying developmental biology, organogenesis, and biomineralization using sea urchin embryos.

My research focuses on how cellular and genetic mechanisms shape embryonic development, with particular interest in live imaging, vesicle dynamics, and calcite skeleton formation.

## Research interests

- Sea urchin development
- Biomineralization
- Organogenesis
- Live embryo imaging
- Vesicle dynamics
- Confocal microscopy
- Quantitative image analysis

## Current work

I use sea urchin embryos to understand how cells coordinate molecular, mechanical, and developmental processes during skeleton formation.
